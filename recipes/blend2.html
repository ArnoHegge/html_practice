Banana Smoothie

Healthy smoothies for weight gain are an excellent calorie-gaining option for those who want to gain weight. They're tasty, simple to prepare, and a great source of vitamins, minerals, and good fats. These delicious smoothie recipes, along with their advantages, can help you gain weight:

Ingredients

  • Greek yogurt - ½ cup
  • Whole milk - 1 cup
  • Peanut butter - 2 tablespoons
  • Large banana - 1
  • Honey (optional) - To taste

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a blender and blend on high speed until thick and smooth.
  2. It’s recommended to add the cocoa powder gradually to prevent caking.

Nutrients

This is the best smoothie for weight gain as it has high potassium, high protein, and healthy fat content which promotes muscular growth and long-lasting energy.
